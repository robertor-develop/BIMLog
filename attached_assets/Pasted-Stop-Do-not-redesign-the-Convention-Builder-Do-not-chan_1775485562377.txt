This is the problem:
The convention system is now powerful, but the user still cannot easily tell:
which version is active,
whether they are looking at the baseline or a re-analysis path,
what evidence has been used so far,
what changed in the last accepted version,
what remains unresolved,
and whether they are on the correct path.

Modify only the Convention Builder flow and the minimum backend support required.
