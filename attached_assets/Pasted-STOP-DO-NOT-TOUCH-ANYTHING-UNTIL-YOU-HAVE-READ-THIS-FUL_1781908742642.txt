We are fixing a real accountability problem in RFIs. Today, RFI creation silently sends an
email via platform SendGrid, swallows any send error, and rfi_ball_in_court_history has zero
writers anywhere. This means the system can imply an RFI was assigned and the recipient holds
the ball, with no defensible record that they were actually notified. This build is the
accountability foundation only.

SCOPE FOR THIS BUILD, EXPLICITLY:
- Do NOT build domain-triggered company capture.
- Do NOT build per-user SendGrid key storage.
- Do NOT build full AI drafting of RFI body text.
- Do NOT build platform SendGrid sending in this build at all — sendEmail() currently swallows
  send errors (confirmed: on SDK failure it logs status "failed" but never throws to the
  caller), so an automated send path cannot be trusted to flip ball-in-court accurately until
  that's fixed separately. Manual confirmation only, this build.
- Do NOT mix send/email workflow state into rfi_ball_in_court_history. That table stays a pure
  custody timeline only — who holds the ball, since when. Nothing about email or delivery.
- Do NOT touch naming_fields. Do NOT modify project 23.
