ARCHITECTURAL INTERIOR FINISHES SPECIFICATION
Project: Audit Final Project
Prepared by: Interior Design Team

1. INTERIOR WALL FINISHES
This document specifies architectural interior finishes for all occupied spaces.

2. FLOORING
- Polished concrete in lobby areas

3. WALL TREATMENTS

Accent wall: Venetian plaster finish in reception

4. CEILING SYSTEMS
Suspended acoustical ceiling tiles: Armstrong Ultima 24x24
Painted gypsum board soffits at 9'-0" AFF in corridors
Exposed structure above with painted mechanical systems in open areas

5. ARCHITECTURAL HARDWARE
Door hardware per AHD-001 schedule
Finish: Satin stainless steel throughout
